Design Verification Audit

  • Fade-Out Effect: Hover out or blur an active element. The tooltip content plays a smooth fade-out and scale-down transition as it hides.
  • Four-Directional Layout: The demo cards showcase top, bottom, left, and right tooltips with arrows automatically rotating to point to triggers.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Use the Tab key to navigate the buttons. Tooltips reveal themselves instantly with focus, styled with custom outlines.
  • Motion Comfort: Toggle prefers-reduced-motion in your system/browser settings to see the transitions and scaling revert to clean, instant state jumps.
