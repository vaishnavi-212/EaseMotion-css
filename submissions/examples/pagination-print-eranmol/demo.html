Sample Content for Print Test

Press Ctrl + P (or Cmd + P on macOS) to open the print preview. The pagination buttons should appear with:

  • Transparent backgrounds (no gradients or heavy colors)
  • No box-shadows or decorative shadows
  • Black or dark gray text for readability on paper
  • Simple borders instead of gradient backgrounds
  • Active page indicated by bold text or underline (not colored background)
