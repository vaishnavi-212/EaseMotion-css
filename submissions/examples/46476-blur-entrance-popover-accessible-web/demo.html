Accessible motion checklist

  • Keyboard first: The native summary trigger can be reached and toggled without a mouse.
  • Visible focus: Focus rings remain high contrast against the panel and page surfaces.
  • Reduced motion: Users who prefer less motion receive an instant, blur-free transition.
